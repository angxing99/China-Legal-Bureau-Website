International notary

 Internationally notarized documents have the legal effect that is discussed within the territory of each country so that the legal acts and activities of such countries can be achieved.As the notarization process is complicated and time-consuming, our experienced international notarization lawyers will ensure that you can get the documents you need efficiently and avoid unnecessary detours.

Procedure of International Notary
First, you need to make an appointment with an international notary attorney to determine the type of notarization and the documents required.If the required files take a long time, you can mention themSend the document by email to an international notary attorney and draft the first draft in advance.

Secondly, on the day of the appointment, you need to bring your valid passport and driver's license or Photo Card for identification.After meeting, the international notarization lawyer will confirm your identity and draft the notarial certificate.The time to draft the notarial certificate depends on the type of document you have certified. If it is short, the notarial service can be completed within the same day, and if it is long, it will take one to two working days.

International notarization, diplomatic certification, iron certification, efficient one-stop professional services, save time and effort.

Common international notary service

Notarization of personal documents:


  • The citizens of Guangzhou are still alive and notarized
  • Notarization of name change
  • Notarization of declaration of renunciation of succession
  • Power of Attorney: Common power of Attorney:
  • Entrust domestic family and friends to deal with the sale of real estate matters
  • Delegate to apply for closing bank account or pension account
  • Entrusting to carry out litigation, etc
